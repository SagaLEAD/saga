About Saglara

Saglara Meyer is a Berkeley Executive Coaching Institute–trained coach who brings together strategic, data-driven thinking with a deeply human-centered practice. Her extensive background in People & Culture and organizational development gives her a rare ability to meet clients exactly where they are.

She specializes in leadership, organizational relationships, career transitions, and women's empowerment — guiding both early-career HR professionals and those re-entering the workforce or beginning an entirely new chapter.

Saglara is also an active member of the Stanford LEAD community, LWHRA, and LISA, and brings her passion for mentorship and community-building into every client engagement.
